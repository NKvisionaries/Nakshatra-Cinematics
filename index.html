Understanding the Craft

Cinematographer vs Film Editor

A Cinematographer paints with light and cameras on set. But a Film Editor is the final storyteller. While one captures the moments, the other breathes life into them.

Video Editor vs Motion Designer

A Video Editor handles the flow and logic of raw footage. A Motion Graphic Artist creates elements that didn't exist—adding typography and VFX to elevate data into cinema.

Sound Design vs Sound Engineer

Sound Design is the art of choosing the perfect "vibe". A Sound Design Engineer is the technical master who mixes these layers for a spatial experience.
